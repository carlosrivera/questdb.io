<h1>Senior DevOps Engineer</h1>

<h2> About QuestDB</h2>

We have built the fastest open source time series database from the ground up to
offer breakthrough performance for real-time analytics using SQL. We bring
experience and technical approaches from low-latency trading to leverage
real-time data processing in various use cases and industries. Our users deploy
QuestDB to make time series analysis fast, efficient, and convenient in
financial services, IoT, application monitoring, and machine learning.

We are a remote-first company based in London and backed by leading venture
capital firms and Y Combinator.

<h2>The role</h2>

As a Senior DevOps Engineer, you will be a critical part of our growing company,
working on a cross-functional team to launch our product as a cloud-hosted
offering. You will oversee the configuration, provisioning, and management of
our cloud service, supporting monitoring, backups, troubleshooting, and disaster
recovery. You have an affinity for best practices in automating operations,
infrastructure security, high-availability systems, and software development.
You'll have the chance to work in an open and collaborative environment to
improve our existing development tools, technology stack, and infrastructure
along the way.

<h2>Responsibilities</h2>

- Implement and maintain a cloud offering for our customers
- Implement monitoring and logging infrastructure
- Own the rollout and deployment to customers
- Design procedures for system troubleshooting and maintenance
- Manage Continuous Integration and Delivery (CI/CD) pipelines with Azure DevOps
  and AWS
- Manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables as part of a
  self-organizing team
- Build tools to reduce occurrences of errors and improve customer experience
- Assist with finding and resolving bugs and other issues
- Provide Level 2 technical support

<h2>Requirements</h2>

- Broad experience with AWS including EC2, S3, ECS, IAM, Lambda, SES,
  Organizations
- Knowledge of cloud architecture design (AWS Well-Architected)
- Experience with CI/CD & Infrastructure as Code (Terraform)
- Experience with containerized applications (Docker, Fargate)
- Experience with automated testing
- Experience with logging and monitoring
- Strong experience managing Linux servers, shell scripting, SSH, etc.
- Familiarity with load balancing/proxies, network architecture and security
- Affinity for software engineering security best practices
- Attention to detail, knowledge of the current state of DevOps and future
  trends
- Excellent written and verbal communication
